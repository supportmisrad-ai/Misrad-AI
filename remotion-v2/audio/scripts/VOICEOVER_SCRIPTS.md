## Background Music

All 60s promo/package videos use: `audio/music/promo-bg.mp3`
- Volume: 0.12
- Fade in: 30 frames (1s)
- Fade out: 45 frames (1.5s)

When voiceover is active, music ducks to 0.06 (handled by AudioLayer).

## שוני v2:
1. **3D space navigation** — camera passes THROUGH frosted glass cards into color-tinted rooms
2. **Each module = a room** — enter/exit via frosted glass pass-through
3. **No slide transitions** — physical camera movement through space
4. **Speed run = continuous fly-through** — 15 frames per room, never stops
5. **Rack focus as navigation** — focus shift = intent to enter
6. **Frosted→clear = selection** — not ripple/glow
7. **Phone as vessel** — camera enters/exits through frosted glass phone
8. **Color-tinted environments** — each module colors the space, not just the header
9. **All numbers = Slot Machine Roll** — no counter animations
10. **Exit macro = 6 blooms emanating** — the phone is alive with 6 modules
